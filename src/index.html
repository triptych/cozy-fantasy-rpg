Cozy Fantasy RPG

Welcome to our game prototype!

This is a simple prototype page for our upcoming cozy fantasy RPG. In this game, players will:

  • Explore a charming fantasy world
  • Build and customize their own cottage
  • Make friends with magical creatures
  • Craft items and cook delicious meals
  • Complete quests to help the local villagers

Stay tuned for more updates as development progresses!
